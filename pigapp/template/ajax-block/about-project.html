Database Idioms

In this site you can browse standard ways of coding database queries, in several popular dialects.

Enthusiast programmers are welcome to participate by writing some implementations.

Content is free and provided as it is. No guarantee is made regarding the quality of the implementations, or the identity of contributors.

User "profiles" are stored in cookies containing nickname and favorites languages. As of 2014 there is no authentication system and no passwords, just provide a nickname if you participate.
